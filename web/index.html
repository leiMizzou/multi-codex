Save each team or subscription into its own slot

  1. Create a slot named after the team, workspace, or subscription you want to preserve.
  2. Open ChatGPT or Settings, then manually switch to the target team or subscription in the browser.
  3. On that slot card, click Launch Codex Login to open a terminal window bound to that slot’s CODEX_HOME.
  4. Complete the login manually, return here, and refresh. That slot will stay isolated and can be aggregated with the rest.
